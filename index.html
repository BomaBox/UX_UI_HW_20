About Me

As a lover of design, a costume artisan, and an avid gamer, I really enjoy coming up with creative solutions to tough problems. That is really what drives me. I’m a people person by nature, and want to know and tell their story. I’m constantly using keen observations, user insights, and background research to create engrossing stories that help fuel immersive designs. Really this all started when I was a little boy and my grandmother would have us do puzzles. Finding the right piece that fits is the goal for any task. I have a passion for gaming and am excited to work in the industry, helping to create thoughtful gaming experiences that support the story and the user.

A career as a costume artisan has made me strong in teamwork, meeting deadlines, iterating constantly, collaboration, and creative problemsolving

Through theatre and roll playing games I have honed my passion for character driven story telling

I’m always one to share what I know and learn from others. Teaching has let me practice that skill to work with different learning styles and delivery techniques so tto ensure the team succeeds
